Before sending me a message, here are some things you should know:

What timezone are you in?
I am currently in the Eastern European Summer Time Zone, Bucharest (GMT+3). However, I am flexible and can accommodate clients from various time zones for meetings and collaborations.
Do we make a contract?
Yes, I provide a contract for all my projects to ensure clear communication and mutual agreement on the scope, timelines, and terms of service. This helps both parties understand the expectations and responsibilities, ensuring a smooth and professional collaboration.
How much do you charge for a website?
The cost of a website varies depending on the complexity, features, and specific requirements of your project. I offer a range of packages to suit different needs and budgets. For a detailed quote, please provide some information about your project, and I will be happy to discuss your needs and provide an estimate.
Do you charge by the hour?
I do not charge by the hour. I prefer to eliminate the stress of counting the hours. Instead, I charge a flat fee per project, regardless of duration.
Are you working with a team?
I work independently, which allows me to provide a personalized and dedicated approach to each project. By handling all aspects of development myself, I ensure a high level of quality and consistency throughout the process.
